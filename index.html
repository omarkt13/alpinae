The problem

"What is this client's total exposure right now?" should not be a project.

When a client holds your fund plus mandates at three outside banks, answering that question means collecting four differently-formatted statements and rebuilding the picture in a spreadsheet. It is slow, it is error-prone, and in a regulated environment a manual error reaching a client — or a regulator — is a serious problem.

01

Drop the statements

Your fund's Excel printout and each custodian's statement, dragged into the client's space. Alpinae reads the account holder on every file and flags any mismatch before it touches a number.

02

Alpinae reconciles

Instrument names are matched across custodians, currencies normalised, valuation dates checked. The result: one consolidated exposure view, with the arithmetic shown.

03

Your report, your format

The consolidated view lands in the reporting format you already send — no manual assembly, nothing to re-learn. You review, you approve, you send.

Data protection & audit

Ready for the questions your compliance officer will ask.

Where is the data?

Switzerland. Compute, database, statement archive, and backups in one Swiss region with a Swiss-owned provider. No US-controlled processor in the chain, no data leaves the jurisdiction — including for any AI processing.

Who can see it?

Role-based access with mandatory two-factor authentication. Advisors see only their assigned clients; compliance sees everything plus the audit trail. Every read is logged — including administrators'.

Can we audit it?

An append-only, tamper-evident log records every statement ingested, every reconciliation, every report generated, and every access — exportable for your own compliance file at any time.

What can it break?

Nothing. Alpinae cannot place orders, move money, or write to any bank, custodian, or system of record — the capability does not exist. Stop using it tomorrow and nothing in your firm changes.

Compute, database, statement archive, audit log, and backups in one Swiss region. Advisors connect read-only over TLS with mandatory two-factor authentication. No processor outside Switzerland — including for AI-assisted document parsing.

⚑ If a statement doesn't match the client space it was dropped into, Alpinae stops and asks you first. One client's positions never appear in another client's report.

By design

What Alpinae is not.

Alpinae is additive by design: it sits on top of what you already run, and asks you to change nothing.

Not a CRM No system-of-record migration, no data entry, no dependency on your CRM — or anyone else's. A lightweight client directory routes statements to the right client space; that's all.
Not a replacement for Excel Your fund printout stays an Excel printout. Your report stays in your format. Alpinae removes the reconciliation labour between the two.
Not a decision-maker Alpinae computes; you approve. Nothing reaches a client or a regulator without your sign-off, and every warning waits for your decision.
Not a client portal — until you say so Internal by default. A read-only client view exists and is switched on per client, only when you decide it should be.
