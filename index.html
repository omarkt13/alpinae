The problem

"What is this client's total exposure right now?" should not be a project.

When a client holds your fund plus mandates at three outside banks, answering that question means collecting four differently-formatted statements and rebuilding the picture in a spreadsheet. Slow, error-prone — and in a regulated environment, a manual error reaching a client or a regulator is a serious problem.

01

Drop the statements

Your fund's Excel printout and each custodian's statement, dragged into the client's space. Alpinae reads the account holder on every file and flags any mismatch before it touches a number.

02

Alpinae reconciles

Instrument names matched across custodians, currencies normalised, valuation dates checked. One consolidated exposure view, with the arithmetic shown.

03

Your report, your format

The consolidated view lands in the reporting format you already send — no manual assembly, nothing to re-learn. You review, you approve, you send.

Data protection & audit

Ready for the questions your compliance officer will ask.

Alpinae is built to be examined. Each claim below maps to a control you can inspect and an artifact you can request.

Ref Your question The control Evidence on request
AL-01 Where is the data? All compute, the database, the statement archive, and backups run in a single Swiss region with a Swiss-owned provider. No US-controlled processor anywhere in the chain; nothing leaves the jurisdiction — including AI-assisted document parsing. Hosting contract & region attestation · sub-processor register
AL-02 Who can see it? Role-based access with mandatory two-factor authentication. Advisors are scoped to their assigned clients; the compliance role reads everything plus the audit trail. Every read is logged — including administrators'. Access-control matrix · read-access log extract
AL-03 Can we audit it? An append-only, hash-chained log records every statement ingested, every reconciliation, every report generated, and every access. Each entry embeds the hash of the previous one — tampering breaks the chain and is evident on verification. Full-log CSV export · chain-verification procedure
AL-04 What can it break? Nothing. Alpinae is read-only: no write path to any bank, custodian, or system of record exists in the software. Decommissioning requires no unwinding — export, delete, done. Architecture note · exit & deletion procedure
AL-05 Can a statement land in the wrong client? Every uploaded file's account holder is read and compared to the client space before anything is parsed. A mismatch blocks reconciliation until a person decides — reassign or quarantine. One client's positions never appear in another client's report. Mismatch flags & resolutions in the audit log · live in the demo

By design

What Alpinae is not.

Alpinae is additive by design: it sits on top of what you already run, and asks you to change nothing.

Not a CRM No system-of-record migration, no data entry, no dependency on your CRM — or anyone else's. A lightweight client directory routes statements to the right client space; that's all.
Not a replacement for Excel Your fund printout stays an Excel printout. Your report stays in your format. Alpinae removes the reconciliation labour between the two.
Not a decision-maker Alpinae computes; you approve. Nothing reaches a client or a regulator without your sign-off, and every warning waits for your decision.
Not a client portal — until you say so Internal by default. A read-only client view exists and is switched on per client, only when you decide it should be.
